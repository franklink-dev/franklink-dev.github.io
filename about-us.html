Our Story

How Franklink Began

As students ourselves, we experienced the frustration of networking firsthand. Sending countless cold emails with minimal responses, struggling to connect with alumni, and watching opportunities slip away.

We knew there had to be a better way. That's when we created Franklink — an AI-powered platform that transforms how students network by finding the right people and crafting authentic, personalized messages that actually get responses.
